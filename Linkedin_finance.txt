Investment Evaluation & Project Valuation
NPV
= Net Present Value
Calculates the present value of expected future cash flows from an investment.

Payback
= Payback Period
Measures how long it takes to recover the initial investment.

Discounted Payback
= Discounted Payback Period
Like Payback Period, but considers the time value of money by discounting future cash flows.

PI
= Profitability Index
Indicates value created per unit of investment; a PI > 1 suggests a profitable project.

DCF
= Discounted Cash Flow
A valuation method based on the present value of future expected cash flows.

IRR
= Internal Rate of Return
The discount rate at which a project’s net present value (NPV) equals zero.

Profitability & Financial Performance
ROE
= Return on Equity
Shows how much profit a company generates from shareholders' equity.

ROI
= Return on Investment
Measures the gain or loss from an investment relative to its cost.

ROA
= Return on Assets
Indicates how effectively a company uses its total assets to generate profit.

ROS
= Return on Sales
Reflects the percentage of revenue that turns into operating profit.

ROIC
= Return on Invested Capital
Shows the return a company generates on all invested capital (debt + equity).

EPS
= Earnings Per Share
Net income allocated to each outstanding share of common stock.

Company Valuation & Market Ratios
P/E
= Price to Earnings
Compares a company's stock price to its earnings per share.

P/BV
= Price to Book Value
Measures the market price of a company relative to its book value.

CAGR
= Compound Annual Growth Rate
The average annual growth rate of an investment over a period of time.

Corporate Finance & Operations
EBITDA
= Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation and Amortization
A measure of operational profitability excluding financing and accounting effects.

FCF
= Free Cash Flow
Cash remaining after capital expenditures, available to investors and creditors.

CAPEX
= Capital Expenditures
Funds used by a company to acquire or upgrade physical assets.

OPEX
= Operating Expenses
Ongoing costs for running the day-to-day operations of a business.

Capital Cost & Valuation Models
WACC
= Weighted Average Cost of Capital
A firm’s average cost of capital from all sources, used as a discount rate.

CAPM
= Capital Asset Pricing Model
A model estimating expected return based on risk-free rate and market risk premium.

Risk & Investment Statistics
Sharpe Ratio
= Sharpe Ratio
Measures risk-adjusted return by comparing excess return to volatility.

VaR
= Value at Risk
Estimates the maximum expected loss over a set time frame at a given confidence level.

Macroeconomics & Inflation Indicators
CPI
= Consumer Price Index
Tracks changes in prices of consumer goods and services – a key inflation measure.

PPI
= Producer Price Index
Measures average changes in prices received by domestic producers – indicates upstream inflation.

Balance of Payments / Trade Balance
= Balance of Payments / Trade Balance
Reflects the flow of goods, services, and capital between countries – affects currency value and risk.
